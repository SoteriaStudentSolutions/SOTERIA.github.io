About Us

 


Soteria  is an innovative and enjoyable program to educate first-year Co-op students on ways to prevent workplace harassment. Our solution features an interactive activity that encourages students to go around campus and locate posters in order to unlock scenarios. The students would have to finish these scenarios to gain points, which provides them with extra incentive to complete the activities as they can receive rewards. Our PSA video and Facebook page inform students and gives them the ability to visit our website to learn more.
